# Pierre Teilhard de Chardin

## Why He Matters
Teilhard saw evolution not as a blind mechanical process but as a directional unfolding toward greater complexity and consciousness. His concept of the noosphere — the thinking layer of the Earth — anticipated the internet, collective intelligence, and the emergence of a planetary mind.

## Connection to This Inquiry
The Civilizational Awakening inquiry is, in many ways, a continuation of Teilhard's question: what becomes possible when human consciousness becomes planetary? His vision of the Omega Point — the convergence of all human intelligence and love — is a north star for the civilization layer of this work.

## Key Ideas
- **Noosphere**: The sphere of human thought that envelops the Earth, analogous to the biosphere.
- **Omega Point**: The hypothetical maximum level of complexity and consciousness toward which the universe evolves.
- **Complexification-Consciousness**: As matter becomes more complex, consciousness increases.
- **Cosmogenesis**: The universe is not a static backdrop but a becoming.
