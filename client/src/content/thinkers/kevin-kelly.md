# Kevin Kelly

## Why He Matters
Kelly's central insight is that technology is not a neutral tool but a living system with its own trajectory and tendencies. His concept of the "technium" — the global system of technology — frames AI not as a product but as an evolutionary force.

## Connection to This Inquiry
Kelly's long-term, non-anxious view of technology's trajectory is a useful counterweight to both techno-utopianism and techno-pessimism. His work on what technology wants informs the civilization layer of this inquiry.

## Key Ideas
- **Technium**: The global, interconnected system of technology that behaves like a living organism.
- **Inevitable**: Certain technological developments are inevitable given the trajectory of the technium.
- **Out of Control**: Complex adaptive systems cannot be fully controlled — they must be cultivated.
- **Technology as Biology**: Technology evolves, adapts, and diversifies like biological life.
