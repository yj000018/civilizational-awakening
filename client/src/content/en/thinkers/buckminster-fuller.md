# Buckminster Fuller

## Why He Matters
Fuller believed that humanity already has enough resources to make every human being comfortable — the problem is design, not scarcity. His concept of the World Game asked: how do we make the world work for 100% of humanity?

## Connection to This Inquiry
Fuller is a direct ancestor of the Civilizational Awakening inquiry. His insistence that civilization is a design problem — not a political or moral problem alone — shapes how this work approaches Next Civ, YOUniverse, and Human Capital Systems.

## Key Ideas
- **Design Science**: The discipline of applying scientific rigor to the design of human systems.
- **World Game**: A simulation framework for solving global resource allocation.
- **Doing More with Less**: Ephemeralization — technology allows us to do ever more with ever less material.
- **Synergetics**: The geometry of thinking and the behavior of whole systems.
