# Sri Aurobindo

## Why He Matters
Aurobindo developed one of the most comprehensive visions of human spiritual evolution in the 20th century. His integral yoga was not a withdrawal from the world but an engagement with it — aimed at transforming matter, life, and mind into expressions of a higher consciousness.

## Connection to This Inquiry
Aurobindo's vision of a supramental consciousness — a mode of knowing that transcends the limitations of the rational mind — is a north star for the Spirit/Soul pillar. His insistence that spiritual evolution must be collective, not just individual, connects to the civilization layer.

## Key Ideas
- **Supramental Consciousness**: A mode of consciousness beyond the rational mind that perceives truth directly.
- **Integral Yoga**: A practice of transformation that includes body, life, mind, and spirit.
- **Evolution of Consciousness**: Consciousness, not matter, is the primary reality; evolution is its unfolding.
- **Life Divine**: The transformation of ordinary human life into an expression of divine consciousness.
