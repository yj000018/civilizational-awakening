# Network / Transmission
## Core question
How do ideas, people and projects connect, inspire and awaken each other?
## Thesis
Ideas do not live only in private notes. They travel through books, essays, conversations, communities, tools, courses, gatherings and relationships.
Network /
 Transmission is the transversal layer that connects the whole inquiry. It is how the body of work becomes shareable, discoverable and alive in relation with others.
## Why it matters
Civilizational Awakening is not a company, product, religion, movement or think tank. But it is not meant to remain isolated either.
The purpose of transmission is to create contact with thinkers, builders, artists, researchers and actors of change exploring human flourishing, consciousness, AI, collective intel
ligence and the future of civilization.

## Design principle
Transmission should not become marketing noise.
It should make the work easier to discover, understand, share and continue with aligned people.
