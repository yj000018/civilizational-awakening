# Actor of Change
## One-line positioning
Actor of Change asks: who are you as an agent of meaningful change?
## Core question
How can a person understand and express their unique contribution to transformation?
## Thesis
People contribute to change in different ways. Some build systems. Some heal. Some teach. Some organize. Some imagine. Some fund. Some connect. Some protect. Some create beauty. Some hold wisdom.
Actor of Change is a framework for mapping these modes of contribution without reducing people to personality types or productivity roles.
## Why it matters
In a time of accelerating transformation, people need more than jobs and identities. They need ways to understand their agency, values, gifts, responsibilities and possible contribution.
Actor of Change supports the passage from self-understanding to meaningful action.
## Relationship to ONEshift
Actor of Change is a core module inside the ONEshift direction.
Where ONEshift explores the broader movement from consciousness to contribution, Actor of Change focuses on the individual: their orientation, gifts, role, maturity, context and path of action.
## Possible dimensions
- values
- gifts
- action style
- contribution archetype
- preferred scale of impact
- relational mode
- domain of service
- maturity path
- collaboration fit
## Design principle
The system should reveal possibility, not label people permanently.
It should help people act with more clarity and alignment, while respecting the mystery and fluidity of human becoming.
## Open questions
- What are the main archetypes of change-making?
- How can contribution be mapped without becoming reductive?
- How can AI help people discover aligned action?
- How does personal transformation become collective contribution?
