# Next Civ
## One-line positioning
Next Civ is a research framework for the next civilization.
## Core question
What systems are needed for humanity to flourish in an age of AI, abundance and planetary-scale intelligence?
## Thesis
The coming decades may transform intelligence, work, longevity, governance, economics, education, creativity and human identity.
Next Civ explores the systems-level questions raised by this transition. It asks what kind of civilization becomes possible when AI, automation, abundance, networks and human potential are no longer marginal forces, but central operating conditions.
## Why it matters
Most discussions about the future focus either on technology or crisis. Next Civ asks a broader question: what would a wiser civilization require?
This includes governance, coordination, trust, abundance, social enterprise, human capital, collective intelligence, spiritual development, multispecies ethics and the design of institutions that support flourishing.
## Research domains
- AI and abundance
- governance and coordination
- collective intelligence
- human potential systems
- trust and reputation
- longevity and meaning
- multispecies civilization
- future education
- social enterprise and contribution
- civilizational memory
## Relationship to Civilizational Awakening
Civilizational Awakening is the wider inquiry.
Next Civ is the research and systems-design layer focused specifically on the future of civilization.
## Open questions
- What comes after scarcity as the main organizing principle?
- What does governance look like in an age of AI-
mediated coordination?
- How can human potential become a civilizational asset without becoming a control system?
- What institutions are needed for an age of abundance and longevity?
- How should humans relate to other species and forms of intelligence?
