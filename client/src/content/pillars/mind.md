# Mind
## Core question
How does intelligence evolve?
## Thesis
The age of AI does not only require better tools. It requires better cognitive infrastructure.
As intelligence becomes distributed across models, agents, automations, documents and knowledge systems, humans need new ways to maintain 
continuity, memory, agency and context.
The Mind pillar explores this missing layer.
## Why it matters
Without cognitive infrastructure, human attention fragments across tools, conversations, files and platforms. Decisions disappear. Context is lost. Memory becomes noisy or incomplete.
The Mind pillar asks how AI can augment human intelligence without dissolving human agency.
