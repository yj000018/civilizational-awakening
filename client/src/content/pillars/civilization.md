# Civilization
## Core question
How do humans flourish together?
## Thesis
The age of AI raises civilizational questions: coordination, governance, abundance, longevity, trust, collective intelligence, 
human potential and the relationship between humans, technology and life.
The Civilization pillar asks how humanity can design systems that help people and societies flourish together, rather than merely optimize extraction, competition or control.
## Why it matters
AI may increase productivity, but productivity alone is not civilization.
A flourishing civilization needs meaning, coordination, legitimacy, care, beauty, governance, memory and shared orientation. It needs systems that make hum
an potential visible and support contribution without reducing people to scores or categories.

## Design principle
Civilizational design must include both systems and soul.
It cannot be only technical governance or economic coordination. It must ask what kinds of beings, relationships, institutions and cultures are worthy of a more abundant future.
